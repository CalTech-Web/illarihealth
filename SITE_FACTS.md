## FAQ Content

**Q: What is Direct Primary Care?**
Illari Health's Direct Primary Care (DPC) model gives you direct access to your physician through a simple flat monthly membership fee of $150, with no insurance involvement. You pay a transparent monthly fee that covers visits, labs, and a comprehensive physical, without co-pays, deductibles, or surprise bills.

**Q: What is the difference between Direct Primary Care and Concierge Medicine?**
Both plans eliminate insurance friction and give you direct physician access. The Concierge Medicine plan ($300/mo) is our premium tier: it includes unlimited visits, an executive-level 90-minute wellness program, an advanced blood panel, modern body composition analysis, direct communication with Dr. Chavez, and 30% off additional labs. The DPC plan ($150/mo) includes up to three visits per month, recommended labs, and a 45-minute comprehensive physical.

**Q: Can I save money if my entire family joins?**
Yes. There is a 10% discount on membership fees when your entire family joins one plan together. An additional 10% discount applies to all plans paid annually instead of monthly.

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
Yes, after the initial 3-month commitment. Any positive balance on your account at cancellation will be applied toward extending your current plan, transferred to another plan, or refunded to you.

**Q: Will I still need health insurance?**
Yes. Your membership covers primary care, labs, and in-office services, but you will still need health insurance for hospitalizations, specialist care, and emergency services. DPC is not a replacement for insurance; it is a better way to access primary care.

**Q: Is there an option for a one-time visit without joining?**
Yes. A single visit without a membership is available for $175. This is a great way to meet Dr. Chavez and experience the practice before committing to a plan.

**Q: What medical conditions do you treat?**
Illari Health offers general primary care for adults and children, sports medicine, clinical and surgical dermatology, and non-operative orthopedics and biologics. Dr. Chavez's 28+ years of training allow the practice to handle a wide range of conditions that typically require a specialist referral elsewhere.

**Q: What if I need to see a specialist?**
Dr. Chavez leverages RubiconMD, a platform connecting members to 200+ specialists for consultation at no extra cost. For PPO members, he refers to vetted in-network specialists. Uninsured patients have access to a curated list of specialists offering cash pricing. Dr. Chavez's training in internal medicine means he works hard to resolve complex cases before a referral is ever needed.

**Q: Is there parking?**
Yes. Free private parking is available immediately south of the building at Western and LeMoyne.

## Key Messaging / Brand Voice

- **Concierge Medicine is the marquee product.** It must be featured prominently, above the fold, as the premium offering. Lead with it. Every page element should create a path toward the Concierge plan.
- **CTA priority: "Call for priority booking."** DPC and Concierge members get priority scheduling. The CTA should communicate urgency and exclusivity, not just "contact us."
- **The goal is to look better and drive to the CTA.** Every design decision should reduce friction between visitor and booking action. Fewer clicks, clearer value, stronger trust signals.
- **Unhurried care is the emotional hook.** Patients are tired of 7-minute appointments, rushed diagnoses, and feeling like a number. Illari Health gives them time with their doctor.
- **Transparency is a competitive weapon.** Flat pricing, no insurance games, no surprise bills. State it plainly and often.
- **Dr. Chavez's 28+ years of experience is the credibility anchor.** He is not a generalist content with the minimum. He trained at Meharry, Rush, and UCLA. He has expertise most family doctors refer out.
- **The practice treats the whole person, not just the complaint.** Prevention, wellness, long-term outcomes. This is the philosophy that differentiates relationship-based medicine from transactional care.
- **Integrity and respect are the core values.** Every message should feel trustworthy, calm, and competent. No hype. No hollow promises. Just a doctor who does what he says.
